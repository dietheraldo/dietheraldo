CPSU Library System

CPSU Hinigaran Campus — Mobile Based Returning/Borrowing

Home Screen

Scan Your ID

Terms & Conditions

A mobile application built with Kodular for CPSU Hinigaran Campus that digitizes the library's book borrowing and returning process. Students scan their QR-coded ID to identify themselves, then scan a book's ISBN to borrow or return it. All transactions are tracked in real time through a PHP and MySQL backend, giving librarians a complete and accurate record of all borrowed and returned books.
